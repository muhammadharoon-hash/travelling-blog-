Our story

Meridian Journal was founded in 2019 by a group of journalists who believed travel writing had become too curated, too sponsored, and too safe. We wanted to go back to the original form: a writer, a notebook, and an honest account of what they actually found.

We accept no press trips, no freebies, and no promotional arrangements. Every story is funded by reader subscriptions and editorial independence is the only thing we won't compromise on.

Our writers have collectively lived in — not just visited — 41 countries. We think that distinction matters. The difference between a week-long itinerary and a year spent in a neighbourhood shows up on the page.
